About CodeCraft

Transforming absolute beginners into elite software engineers through immersive coding education.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, CodeCraft was built on a simple premise: traditional education isn't keeping up with the rapid pace of the tech industry. We decided to bridge that gap by creating intensive, hands-on bootcamps.

Today, our graduates are thriving at innovative startups and leading companies around the globe.

Our Methodology

  • Project-Based: Theory is good, building is better. You will write code every single day.
  • Code Reviews: Get line-by-line feedback from senior engineers to write clean, professional code.
  • Career Support: Resume polishing, mock interviews, and direct referrals to hiring partners.
